In a Box is a company that delivers food boxes to your home. Customers receive food boxes with all the fresh ingredients you need to make deliciouse and quality recipes.

There are three packages

  • Beginner Package
  • Everyday Package
  • Deluxe Package
for you to choose and one custom package which can be arranged by yourself.

We also have services and products offers such as
